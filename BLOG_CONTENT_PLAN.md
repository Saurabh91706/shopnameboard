## Common Installation Challenges and Solutions

### Challenge 1: Weak Wall Structure
**Solution**: Use MS frame support anchored to multiple points. For very weak walls, consider freestanding boards.

### Challenge 2: No Electrical Point (for lit boards)
**Solution**: Run concealed wiring from the nearest point. Budget additional ‚Çπ1,500-‚Çπ3,000 for this.

### Challenge 3: Municipal Objections
**Solution**: Work with manufacturers experienced in local regulations. Some areas prohibit projecting boards or have size restrictions.

### Challenge 4: Monsoon Installation
**Solution**: Avoid installation during heavy rains. Waterproofing is crucial in coastal cities like Mumbai and Chennai.
